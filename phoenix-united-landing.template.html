Phoenix United was built by two brothers — Bader and Amer Al-Akkad. The idea came from Bader's own story: when illness ended his playing career, he came back and built the club he wished had existed — one where professional football and a real education are part of the same path.

We hold one of the few professional licences the UAE FA has granted. We use it to build players who go further on the pitch and arrive ready for everything beyond it.

Come for the football — everyone does. You'll leave with more than you came with: a degree, a network, a future you can build on.
